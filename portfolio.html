About

We are a team of global scientist based in Africa doing a research on how to eliminate common problems faced on a day to day life by people of all classes and race and coming up with possible immediate solutions for them

Projects

Below is a list of some of the projets we have undertaken as we continued on our research.

Socia Economic Activities. We have analysed the various activities that the communities are involved in to increase their economic satus and improve on their living standards and chose the below to assist them.

  • Bore holes

    We have dung several boreholes to aid in easy access of clean water so that the women and children can easily get water for their domestic use and easily water their crops and animals.

  • Animal husbandry

    We provide capital for the communities to start small projects eg chicken rearing, goat farming for milk and meat supply. They can aslo be sold to increase family cash flow.

  • Educational Materials

    We provide learning materials for the communities to learn new ideas eg business. We also provide them with spiritual learning materials like bibles and bible study materials.
